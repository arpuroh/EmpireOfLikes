Prologue: Twilight of the Producers

1965: Sparks, rivets, and shift whistles. The might of industry.

2025: Ring lights, brand deals, and collab dances. The allure of attention.

When a civilisation’s prime export becomes attention, decadence ceases to be a moral failing and becomes the P&L strategy.

Factories once defined national pride; now “creator houses” do. Data nugget: manufacturing employment ↓ 29% since 1990; influencer economy valued at $250 bn.

“Rome paid its soldiers in silver; we pay ours in followers.”
